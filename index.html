Who am I?

I am a recent graduate of the Davis Technical College's Information Technology program looking to enter the field of technology. I have always had my sights set on broadening my understanding of the world around me and for the last several years technology has been my aspiration. I have taken every class within my reach and latched onto the challenge and knowledge available to me. Within the four years of my highshool experience I took classes through four different entities, these include: Farmington High School, Davis Catalyst Center, Weber State University, and Davis Technical College. While learning from these institutions I also dabbled with self-hosting and personal projects where I have continued to obtain a more functional knowledge of the technology I use every day.
